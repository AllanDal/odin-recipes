Pasta

Pasta is a beloved staple in many cuisines around the world. This versatile dish can be enjoyed in numerous ways, from a simple tomato sauce to more elaborate recipes with a variety of ingredients. Here’s a basic recipe to get you started with classic pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 12 ounces of your choice (e.g., spaghetti, penne)
  • Olive Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Tomato Sauce: 1 can (15 ounces)
  • Parmesan Cheese: 1/2 cup, grated
  • Fresh Basil: A handful, chopped
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes for heat

How to Make Classic Pasta Step by Step

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add tomato sauce to the skillet and simmer for about 5 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.
  4. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce, stirring to coat the pasta evenly.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan and basil if desired.
